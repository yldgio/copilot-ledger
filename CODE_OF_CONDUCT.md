# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected behavior

- Be welcoming and professional.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Give and receive feedback respectfully.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing sensitive data.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Trolling, repeated disruption, or bad-faith participation.
- Any conduct that would make the project unsafe or unwelcoming.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or take other reasonable action to protect the project community.

If you need to report conduct concerns, use the repository's private contact channel if one is available, or contact a maintainer privately.
